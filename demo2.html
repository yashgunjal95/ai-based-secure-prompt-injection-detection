How It Works
Every prompt passes through three independent detection layers before reaching the AI agent.
L1 Rule Engine — 77 regex patterns catch known signatures like "ignore all instructions", DAN templates, and prompt-leaking attempts in <0.5ms.
L2 ML Classifier — TF-IDF + Logistic Regression catches paraphrased and novel attack phrasing that evades rules.
L3 LLM Validator — Groq llama-3.3-70b performs semantic analysis, catching zero-day jailbreaks and social engineering.
Final score = (L1 × 0.30) + (L2 × 0.35) + (L3 × 0.35)
Override rules can only raise the score, never lower it.
